Linear Equations & Coordinate Geometry

Slope

$$m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1}$$

Point–Slope Form

$$y - y_1 = m(x - x_1)$$

Slope–Intercept Form

$$y = mx + b$$

Midpoint

$$M = \left(\frac{x_1+x_2}{2},\frac{y_1+y_2}{2}\right)$$

Distance

$$d = \sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2 + (y_2-y_1)^2}$$

Quadratics

Standard Form

$$y = ax^2 + bx + c$$

Vertex Form

$$y = a(x-h)^2 + k$$

Factored Form

$$y = a(x-r_1)(x-r_2)$$

Quadratic Formula

$$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$$

Discriminant

$$D = b^2 - 4ac$$

Axis of Symmetry

$$x = -\frac{b}{2a}$$

Exponents & Radicals

Product Rule

$$a^m a^n = a^{m+n}$$

Quotient Rule

$$\frac{a^m}{a^n} = a^{m-n}$$

Power Rule

$$(a^m)^n = a^{mn}$$

Radical Product

$$\sqrt{ab} = \sqrt{a}\sqrt{b}$$

Fractional Exponent

$$a^{1/n}=\sqrt[n]{a}$$

Polynomial Factoring

Difference of Squares

$$a^2-b^2=(a-b)(a+b)$$

Sum of Cubes

$$a^3+b^3=(a+b)(a^2-ab+b^2)$$

Difference of Cubes

$$a^3-b^3=(a-b)(a^2+ab+b^2)$$

Functions

Average Rate of Change

$$\frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}$$
